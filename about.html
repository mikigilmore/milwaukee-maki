About Us

Starting out as two Japanese immigrants serving home-cooked meals out of their kitchen, Milwaukee Maki has grown into Milwaukeeans' favorite spot for Japanese food. Ranked #3 consistently for best Japanese food in the state since 2001, we focus on delivering classic Japanese dishes with fresh ingredients and cooking methods that have lasted the test of time.
