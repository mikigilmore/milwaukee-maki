Milwaukee Maki, the best Japanese food in the "tuna-verse".

Come get a taste of Japan at our cozy location in Milwaukee, Wisconsin, right by the beautiful river. From traditional noodle soup bowls to more creative sushi rolls, we've got everything to satisfy your craving. You'll "dolphinately" love it!

Are you a lover of fish puns? Take the fish joke challenge! If you can make our stoic Chef Yoshida chuckle with one of your fish-related jokes, he'll treat you to a free mochi ice cream.
